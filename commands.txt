Current user:whoami
Trying to get access to the passwd file:cat /etc/passwd
Trying to get access to the shadow file:cat /etc/shadow
Getting SUID bit files:find / -type f -perm -04000 -ls
Getting SGID bit files:find / -type f -perm -02000 -ls
Find world readable files in /home:find /home/ -perm -4 -type f -exec ls -al {} \;
Find password strings in files within /var/www:grep -R 'password' /var/www/
Output all bash history files:grep -R . /home/*/.bash_history
